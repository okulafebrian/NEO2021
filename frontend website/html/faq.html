Frequently Asked Questions

What is NEO?

NEO is an annual event from Bina Nusantara English Club (BNEC), where high school students from all over Indonesia will compete in 5 fields of competition (Debate, Speech, Podcast, Newscasting, Short Story Writing). This year’s National English Olympics will be held on 15th - 20th of November 2021 with the theme “Luminescence”.

Where will NEO 2021 be conducted?

NEO 2021 will be conducted online via ZOOM.

What is the age qualification to join?

The age qualification to join are from 15 to 22 years old.

Can junior high school participate?

Yes, but please pay close attention to the participants and the level of difficulties, because our competitions mainly focused on high schoolers and university students. Exception for the Debate competition, junior high school students are not allowed to participate.

Can I join 2 competition fields at the same time?

Yes, you may join 2 competition fields at the same time. However, keep in mind that you have to prepare yourself for the competition fields’ schedule.

Can the past champion of NEO join the same field competition again?

Yes, you can either participate in the same field as you did before or you can choose another field to participate in.

How many participants are allowed to join in each field for each institution?

There is no limit. Each institution can register as many participants as they want.

How do I request an invitation letter for my institution?

You can request an invitation letter by clicking the “Request Here” button in the Contact section of the website, there you will fill in the necessary information regarding your institution.

May I invite any supporters to see the National English Olympics?

No observer is allowed to partake in the whole activity of the National English Olympics.

If I want to register for more than 1 participant, how many accounts should I have?

There are two options available, registration as an individual or as an institution.

  • If you want to register yourself as an individual participant, you have to create one account for every identity.
  • If you want to register yourself through your institution, you only create one account that can be used to register several participants.
How and when will I know that my institution is officially registered?

Your institution will receive the official announcement from us via email a maximum of 48 hours of working days after you complete the payment on our NEO website.

Can I secure my spot by giving partial payment?

We are sorry, but we do not accept any partial/down payment. You need to pay your entire fee to secure your spot.

If I cancel the registration, will I get a full refund?

No, the committee will only provide 50% of the refund for any kind of payment. As an exception, if you’re canceling your registration D-14 from the technical meeting, you won’t get any refund at all.

Can I edit my participant’s data after I register?

PIC’s and participant’s data can only be edited until D-14 from the technical meeting, November 13th, 2021. The committee will print the name stated on the website for the certificates.
